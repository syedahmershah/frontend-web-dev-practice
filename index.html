How It Works

This temperature converter lets you convert a given temperature from one unit to another. Simply enter the temperature value, choose the unit you’re converting from and the unit you want to convert to, then hit the "Convert" button.

The conversion is done by first converting the value to Celsius and then converting it to the desired unit using standard formulas.
